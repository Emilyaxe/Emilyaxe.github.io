General

Jingjing Liang is a Postdoctoral Researcher at the School of Software Engineering, East China Normal University, where her collaborating supervisor is Prof. Ting Su. She completed her Ph.D. at Peking University in January 2022, where she was advised by Prof. Yingfei Xiong. Prior to that, she obtained her Bachelor's degree from East China Normal University in 2016.

Her current research focuses on compiler testing, leveraging Large Language Models (LLMs) to ensure compiler quality and performance improvement. During her Ph.D., her research interests were mainly in fault localization, program repair, and patch review.
